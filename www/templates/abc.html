Basic emergency care consists of the ABC's

Airway: Means air passage. The goal of maintaing an open airway is to ensure an unobstructed flow of air in and out of the lungs


Breathing: Means the ability to provide oxygen to the blood by the lungs.


Circulation: Means the presence of signs of circulation and pulse (skin colour, tone, temperature, texture and condition.

1 STAY SAFE don't venture into an unkown situation without first knowing if it is safe


2 STAY CALM don't panic


3 TAKE CHARGE be assertive, yet compassionate


4 LOOK for witnesses and for what might have caused the illness of injury


5 DETERMINE unresponsiveness and check for breathing


6 CALL 911


7 Open the airway


8 CHECK for breathing
