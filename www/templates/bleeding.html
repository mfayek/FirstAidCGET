Loss of blood and wounds can be caused by various mechanisms result in the breaking of soft tissues in the body and if not stopped it will lead to shock.

What to do

1 Direct Pressure:

Apply direct pressure to stop the bleeding


2 Rest:

Keep the injured person at rest to reduce the amount of circulating blood at the site.


3 Reasure:

Reassure the injured person and treat for shock

Nose Bleed

1 Have the person sit with the head slightly forward. (Not tilted back, since this will cause the blood to drain in to the throat and stomach and may cause vomiting).


2 Pinch the nose to control and stop the bleeding and place an ice pack on the nose.


3 Place a small gauze or pad under the nostrils to absorb any dripping blood.
