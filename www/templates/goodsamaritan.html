• Identify yourself

• Ask for consent

• Act in good faith

• Use common sense

• Offer to help

In any event, any first aider who initiates CPR and/or First Aid should continue until:

• The person’s breathing and signs of circulation have been restored;


• Care is transferred to another trained person who continues CPR or First Aid;


• Care is transferred to EMS personnel or to a licensed physician;


• The first aider is too exhausted to continue resuscitation;


• The first aider life is in danger.
