The First-Aider should:

1 Keep yourself and the person (s) safe and out of danger


2 Try to remain calm (body language)


3 Take charge and get assistance


4 Call 911 after stopping major bleeding


5 Keep focus on the basics (ABC's - Airway, Breathing, Circulation)


6 Rapid body check and stop major bleeding


7 Treat for shock


8 Always have the door open and accessible for EMS
